Welcome To Lexa

Lexa is a library of courses. Each course is comprised of bite-sized lessons that are designed to be completed on your commute, while you wait in line, or whenever you have some time to spare. Lexa is an app for people who want to use their free time well.

To get started, head on over to the library and pick out something to learn! Once you select a course, all courses you are currently taking will be displayed on your home page.
